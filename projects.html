PROJECT 01

MeetNote

In Development — FYP
TypeMobile + Backend
ModuleEE016-3-3-PP1
SupervisorDr. Sharman Sundarajoo

My Final Year Project: a Flutter and FastAPI mobile application that records meetings and generates professional Minutes of Meeting reports using local large language models run through Ollama, entirely offline. The aim is to give teams a private, dependable way to capture decisions without sending audio to third-party cloud services.

The planned enhancement is in-app audio recording with speaker diarization, using Whisper for transcription and Pyannote.audio on the backend to separate and label individual speakers — turning a flat transcript into a structured, attributable record of who said what.

Flutter FastAPI Ollama Whisper Pyannote.audio sqflite
PROJECT 02

Pulsense

In Development — GDP
TypeWearable + Mobile
ModuleEE045-4-3
Team5-person group

A wearable neurorehabilitation system built as a Group Development Project with teammates Jushita Pediredla, Abdelrahman Ibrahim, Rodaina Ahmed, and Mohammed Fairoos. The system pairs an ESP32-based sensor glove, fitted with heart rate, galvanic skin response, and flex sensors, with a Flutter mobile frontend and a FastAPI backend running on Ollama for local inference.

My individual contribution was the Flutter mobile frontend, including real-time data polling from the glove's heart rate stream and integration of flex sensor readings into the patient monitoring view.

Flutter ESP32 FastAPI Ollama HR / GSR Sensors
